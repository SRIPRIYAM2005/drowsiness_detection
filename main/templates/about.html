How it Works

Our AI Drowsiness Detection System is a sophisticated safety tool designed to prevent road accidents caused by driver fatigue. By combining Computer Vision and Deep Learning, the system monitors physical cues in real-time to determine if a driver is losing focus.

The Core Metrics

1. EAR (Eye Aspect Ratio)

Using MediaPipe, we map 468 facial landmarks. EAR calculates the distance between eyelids. If the ratio drops below a threshold, the system knows the eyes are closing.

2. PERCLOS

Percentage of Eye Closure. This measures the fraction of time the eyes are more than 80% closed over a specific interval. It is the most reliable indicator of long-term fatigue.

How This Helps You

Driving while tired is often compared to driving under the influence. This application acts as a Digital Co-Pilot that:

  • Provides instant auditory/visual alerts during micro-sleeps.
  • Tracks fatigue trends over long journeys via the Analytics Dashboard.
  • Identifies high-risk hours in your driving schedule.

⚠️ Driver Safety Warning

This application is an assistive tool, not a replacement for human judgment.

Technology can fail due to poor lighting, camera angles, or hardware lag. Always follow these rules:

  • If you feel tired, stop driving immediately, even if the app says "IDLE."
  • Do not rely on the app to "keep you awake."
  • Ensure your camera has a clear, well-lit view of your face.
  • Take a 15-minute break every 2 hours of driving.

Stay Safe. Stay Alert.
