Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about the AI Drowsiness Detection system.

How does the system detect drowsiness? ▾
The system uses MediaPipe to track 468 facial landmarks. It specifically monitors the Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR). When your eyes remain closed or partially closed for a sustained period (PERCLOS), the AI triggers a fatigue alert.
Does it work at night or in low light? ▾
Accuracy depends on your camera's quality. While the AI is robust, it requires a clear view of your eyes. For night driving, using an Infrared (IR) camera or ensuring cabin lights are on will significantly improve detection.
What should I do if the alarm goes off? ▾
Stop driving immediately. The system is designed to catch "micro-sleeps." If an alert is triggered, it means your fatigue levels are dangerously high. Pull over at the nearest safe location and rest.
Why is the Status showing "Idle"? ▾
The status shows "Idle" when the camera feed is not started or if the AI cannot find a face in the frame. Ensure you are positioned centrally in front of the camera and have clicked "Start Monitoring."
Is my video data being recorded? ▾
No. The system processes the video stream locally and in real-time. Only the numerical metrics (like EAR and PERCLOS timestamps) are saved to the dashboard for your personal analytics history.
